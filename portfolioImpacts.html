How Does This Information Impact Personal Investments

The impacts from this data are minimal. This project was started as an exploration of measuring volatility in the amplitude of the Fourier versus measuring volatility in standard deviation. While they seem to be correlated, it is unknown where and when these two statistics diverge.

In particular, volatility drag can cause stocks to underperform, particularly leveraged stocks like $SSO. This is because if your security decreases in price by 1%, then increases by 1%, your security is worth less than when you started (the same goes in the opposite manner). This drag can become formidable for long-term investors to overcome.
